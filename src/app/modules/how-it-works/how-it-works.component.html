Turn Every Scan into an Experience

At Share a Scan, we make custom QR merchandise simple and fun. Whether you’re a creator, brand, or small business, our process ensures your personalized hoodies, mugs, or phone cases are both stylish and smart. Follow these easy steps and bring your scannable story to life.

Discover the Code

Start by choosing your favorite product from our collection — hoodies, drinkware, caps, and more. Upload your unique QR code or generate one directly on our platform. This code can link to your website, digital profile, or any online space that tells your story.

Scan the Qr Code

Each product is printed with a high-quality, durable QR design. Just scan it with any smartphone camera to instantly access the digital experience behind your merch — a social profile, a shop link, or even a playlist.

Open the content

Your QR takes people directly to your chosen content — quick, seamless, and interactive. From creators sharing their portfolios to brands connecting with customers, every scan becomes a new way to engage.
